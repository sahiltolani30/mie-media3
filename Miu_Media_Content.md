## 1. About Miu Media
Miu Media is a digital marketing and brand design agency founded by Dipansh. They specialize in helping architects and interior designers attract high-value clients in 90 days, guaranteed. Their core focus is transforming creative studios into authoritative brands that inspire trust and desire, moving them beyond simply showcasing portfolios.

**Founder's Story:**
At just 21 years old, Dipansh has already built a unique path. Starting at 17 by selling digital art online, he pivoted into brand design. While working as a marketing intern at an architecture firm, he realized that architects and interior designers were brilliant at building physical spaces but struggled to build their own digital presence. This insight sparked the creation of Miu Media.

## 5. Proof of Work / Case Studies

### Case Study 1: From ₹0 to 24 Warm Leads in 7 Days
**Client:** Mumbai-based Interior Design Studio
- **Challenge:** Instagram engagement wasn’t translating into leads, and previous ads were expensive and attracted unqualified prospects.
- **Solution:** 
  - Created a “Free 30-Minute Home Transformation Consultation” offer.
  - Launched an Instagram-only Meta Campaign targeting local homeowners.
  - Built a high-converting landing page and implemented fast WhatsApp follow-ups.
- **Results:** 24 warm leads with just ₹4,200 (~$50) in ad spend, leading to 4 consultations and 2 closed projects worth several lakhs within 48 hours.

### Case Study 2: From 35K to 230K+ Views in 30 Days
**Client:** Studio Moon (Interior Design Studio)
- **Challenge:** Stuck at ~35K monthly views, primarily from existing followers with no system for new leads.
- **Solution:** Produced SEO-rich Reels using only project visuals (no face, no voiceover) and built a bio/caption funnel.
- **Results in 1 Month:** Views skyrocketed to 237,945 (+580%), reaching 147,270 accounts (95% non-followers).

### Case Study 3: Automated Lead Capture for HOD
**Client:** HOD (Interior Design Studio)
- **Challenge:** Manually handling Instagram inquiries led to delays and missed follow-ups.
- **Solution:** Automated responses for DMs containing "design" or "consultation", directing them to a structured Google Form for qualification.
- **Results:** Response time cut to zero, 100% of inquiries captured with complete project details, and the studio team was freed from repetitive DM handling.
